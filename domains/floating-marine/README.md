# Floating & Marine Systems — community charter

An Open Deck community of practice for **floating and marine systems** —
station-keeping, motions, and marine operations on offshore assets. Public,
open, non-proprietary. Several topics here can run real analysis today; the rest
are discussion-first.

## Who it's for
Naval architects, mooring and riser engineers, marine-operations and
installation engineers, and floating-systems generalists.

## What it covers
- Mooring & station-keeping — catenary and taut systems, anchors, line design.
- Risers — catenary risers, jumpers, VIV, riser fatigue.
- Hydrodynamics — RAOs, diffraction, seakeeping, motions, wave loads.
- Vessels & floaters — hull and marine analysis, vessel selection and suitability.
- Installation & marine operations — lifts, rigging, mudmat/foundation install.
- LNG — carriers, sloshing, terminals and transfer systems.
- Offshore renewables fold in here for now (until the area has its own mass).

## How it answers (read before posting)
- **Runnable where we can, discussion-first elsewhere.** Mooring, riser,
  hydrodynamics, installation and vessels have worked examples we can run;
  LNG and renewables are discussion-first. When something needs proper
  analysis, we say so and outline what it would take.
- **Assumptions before numbers.** Every quantitative reply states its
  assumptions (3–5 lines) up front; headline numbers are sanity-checked ranges,
  not point claims. Anything substantial ends with an Assumptions & Sensitivity
  note.
- **Open material only.** No client data, no licensed or proprietary content,
  no NDA-bound drawings. When in doubt, keep it general.
- **In domain.** Off-domain asks get a friendly one-line redirect; IT/computer
  questions are not this channel.
