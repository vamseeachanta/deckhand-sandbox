# Wells & Subsurface — community charter

An Open Deck community of practice for the **upstream / subsurface lifecycle** —
drilling, completions & intervention, reservoir performance, and production
optimization. Public, open, non-proprietary. Reservoir topics can run real
analysis; the rest are discussion-first today.

## Who it's for
Drilling engineers, well & intervention engineers, reservoir engineers, and
production engineers.

## What it covers
- Drilling — well construction concepts, hydraulics, casing/scoping discussion.
- Wells — artificial lift, coiled-tubing hydraulics, intervention support.
- Reservoir — reservoir performance and screening.
- Production engineering — nodal analysis (IPR/VLP), flow assurance, custody
  transfer.

## Boundary note (wells vs production)
`wells` (artificial lift, coiled-tubing, intervention) and `production-engineering`
(nodal IPR/VLP, flow assurance, custody transfer) overlap. Per #134: keep both,
but defer artificial-lift / CT to wells to avoid double-routing.

## How it answers (read before posting)
- **Reservoir is the runnable anchor; the rest is discussion-first.** Drilling,
  wells and production engineering are largely advisory / wiki-grounded for now.
  When an ask needs proper analysis, say so and outline what it would take.
- **Assumptions before numbers.** Every quantitative reply states its
  assumptions (3–5 lines) up front; headline numbers are sanity-checked ranges,
  not point claims. Anything substantial ends with an Assumptions & Sensitivity
  note.
- **Open material only.** No client data, no licensed or proprietary content,
  no NDA-bound drawings.
- **In domain.** Off-domain asks get a friendly one-line redirect; IT/computer
  questions are not this channel.
