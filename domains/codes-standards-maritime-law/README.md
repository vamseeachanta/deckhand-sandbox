# Codes, Standards & Maritime Law — community charter

A cross-cutting Open Deck community of practice — the **advisory layer every
other community leans on**: engineering codes & standards navigation, plus
maritime/regulatory context. Public, open, non-proprietary. Advisory and
discussion-first by nature.

## Who it's for
Any offshore/marine engineer who needs to find the right code clause, compare
standards, or understand the regulatory and maritime-law context around a
design — and the champions of the other communities when a code question
crosses topics.

## What it covers
- Engineering codes & standards — navigating DNV, API, ABS, ISO, IEC and IMO
  instruments; which standard governs, edition differences, how a clause is
  applied.
- Class & regulatory context — classification society and flag-state basics,
  IMO conventions at a working level.
- Maritime / admiralty law — general orientation on the legal context of
  offshore and marine operations.

## How it answers (read before posting)
- **Advisory, not a ruling.** This community points you to the governing code,
  clause and edition and explains intent in plain terms — it does not certify a
  design or substitute for a Class review.
- **Not legal advice.** Maritime-law discussion is general orientation only, not
  a legal opinion; for anything consequential, consult qualified counsel.
- **Cite the source.** Name the standard, clause and edition behind any guidance
  so it can be checked.
- **Open material only.** No client data, no licensed standard text reproduced
  in full (point to the clause, don't paste copyrighted text), no NDA-bound
  documents.
- **In domain.** Off-domain asks get a friendly one-line redirect; IT/computer
  questions are not this channel.
